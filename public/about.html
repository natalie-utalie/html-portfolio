About Me

I am a language teacher with a master's degree in pedagogy and over seven years of diverse instructional experience. Being fluent in English and Russian, I am adept at teaching both children and adults.

My teaching journey began with a childhood love for languages. I was always fascinated by the idea of understanding people from around the world, which led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Linguistics. It was during my time as a tutor and volunteer in university projects that I discovered my true passion for teaching. This transformative experience inspired me to shift my focus to pedagogy, eventually earning a Master's degree in the field. Studying at university not only deepened my love for teaching but also equipped me with crucial academic skills necessary for educating people effectively.

In addition, I'm diving into the exciting world of front-end web development. So, I hope to become a linguist who can decode both human and machine languages.
